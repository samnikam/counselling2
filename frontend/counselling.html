How We Help

Guidance for What You're Going Through

Confidential counselling for stress, studies, career confusion and personal well-being — for every student of Anantnag district.

Stress & Well-being

Talk about exam pressure, anxiety and emotional health in a safe, confidential space.

Study Guidance

Get help with study habits, focus, time management and academic difficulties.

Career Confusion

Not sure what to do after 10th, 12th or graduation? Talk it through with a counsellor.

Complete Confidentiality

Counselling-related information is protected with proper login systems and data security.

The Process

Booking a Session Is Simple

01

Request Counselling

Fill the request form below with your details and what you'd like help with.

02

Get a Time Slot

The Counselling Center confirms your appointment and shares the session details.

03

Attend Your Session

Meet your counsellor — in person at the center or through your school/college.

04

Continued Support

Follow-up sessions and guidance whenever you need them. You're never on your own.

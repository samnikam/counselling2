Our Purpose

One Platform. Every Student.

The District Administration of Anantnag is establishing a Counselling and Youth Development Center — and this portal is its digital backbone.

The main purpose of this portal is simple: to create a single digital platform that connects students, institutions and administration for learning, interaction, counselling and overall development.

It supports students in mental well-being, career guidance and overall development — making sure the right help reaches the right student at the right time.

The Need

Why Anantnag Needs This Portal

At present, there is no single system where all students and institutions are connected. Some key gaps we observed:

No Central Platform

Schools and colleges work separately, with no shared system connecting all students of the district.

Limited Counselling Access

Students often do not know where to go for help, guidance or emotional support.

Missed Opportunities

Events, scholarships and programs go unnoticed because information doesn't reach every student.

Disconnected Alumni

Alumni have no structured way to mentor and guide current students.

Why This Portal

One Platform for the Whole District

Today, schools and colleges work separately, and students often do not know where to go for help, guidance or opportunities. Important information gets missed, and support arrives too late.

This portal changes that — bringing every student, institution and the District Administration onto a single connected platform for learning, interaction, counselling and growth.

How the Portal Works

2

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked

Who can use this portal?

All students of the 78 Higher Secondary Schools and 10 Colleges of Anantnag district, along with alumni, institutions and the District Administration.

How do I get my student login?

Student logins will be issued through your school or college once registration opens. Announcements will be shared on this website and through institutions.

Which languages does the portal support?

The portal is planned to support English, Hindi and Urdu.

Is my counselling information safe?

Yes. A proper login system and data protection are maintained, with special care for counselling-related information.

What We Offer

Everything the Portal Brings Together

A complete set of services, built around students, institutions, alumni and the District Administration — all reachable from one place.

Service 01

Psychological Counselling & Mental Wellness

Evidence-based individual counselling for mental health conditions — trauma-informed, culturally sensitive care in your language. Sessions of 45–60 minutes.

  • Clinical PTSD assessment & structured trauma recovery
  • CBT for anxiety and depression
  • Grief and bereavement support
  • Psychiatric consultation & GMC Anantnag referral
Who is this for

Any resident of Anantnag district experiencing emotional or mental-health difficulties. All ages welcome.

In-Person · Online · Phone Book Appointment →
Service 02

Career & Aptitude Guidance

Scientific aptitude assessment and expert guidance at key academic and career junctures — from Class 10 stream selection to professional transitions.

  • JKSSB, JKPSC & UPSC pathway guidance
  • Entrance exam guidance (NEET, JEE, CLAT, CAT)
  • Entrepreneurship & self-employment counselling
  • Resume preparation & interview readiness
Who is this for

Students from Class 10 onward, graduates, and young job-seekers planning their next step.

In-Person · Online Book Appointment →
Service 03

Educational & Academic Guidance

Comprehensive academic support from Class 11 through post-graduation, with special attention to first-generation learners and rural students across all tehsils.

  • Scholarships: PMSSS, PM YASASVI, Minority schemes
  • Higher-education planning in India and abroad
  • Learning-disability identification & referral
  • Examination stress & performance anxiety
Who is this for

Students from Class 11 to post-graduate level, and parents seeking academic support for their children.

In-Person · Online Book Appointment →
Service 04

Group Therapy & Peer Support

Facilitated group sessions in the dedicated 32 sq.m Group Therapy Room, with 6–10 participants. New batches commence every six weeks.

  • Conflict & trauma recovery groups (18–30)
  • Peer-support circles for students
  • Stress & anxiety management workshops
  • Adolescent well-being & life-skills groups
Who is this for

Young people and adults who benefit from shared healing, peer connection and a supportive group setting.

In-Person Book Appointment →
Service 05

Family Counselling

Structured counselling for families affected by conflict, grief, displacement and communication breakdown — for individual members or the whole family together.

  • Conflict-related family trauma & healing
  • Grief support for conflict-affected families
  • Parent–child communication & repair
  • Support for families of those with mental-health conditions
Who is this for

Families and individual family members in Anantnag seeking to rebuild communication and cope with difficult events.

In-Person · Online Book Appointment →
Service 06

Vocational & Skill Development

Assessment and guidance for vocational pathways, ITI, Skill India, self-employment and government-scheme access for youth seeking work in J&K.

  • ITI & polytechnic course selection
  • Skill India & PMKVY programme access
  • Self-employment & startup guidance
  • Government-scheme & apprenticeship access
Who is this for

Youth seeking vocational training, skilling and real employment opportunities across Jammu & Kashmir.

In-Person · Online Book Appointment →
Inside the Portal

Every Feature, in One Place

From your profile and dashboard to counselling, events and secure multilingual access — here is everything the portal brings together for students, institutions and administration.

Feature 01

Student Login & Profile

Each student receives a personal login ID, with basic details such as school or college and class maintained securely in the system.

Feature 02

Student Dashboard

See notices and announcements, events and competitions, important updates and counselling information — all on one personal screen.

Feature 03

Interaction Platform

A structured discussion space where students can ask questions, share ideas and learn from one another across the district.

Feature 04

Gallery & Video Section

Schools and colleges upload photos and videos of events, while students share presentations and activities with the wider community.

Feature 05

Talent Showcase

Students display their skills, projects and achievements — building motivation, recognition and confidence along the way.

Feature 06

Events & Activities

District-level events and competitions are listed, students register online, and workshops and awareness programs are managed in one place.

Feature 07

Counselling System

Request counselling, book appointments and get guidance for stress, studies and career — directly supporting the Counselling Center initiative.

Feature 08

Career Guidance

Information about career options, guidance sessions and basic skill-development resources to help students plan their next steps.

Feature 09

Institution Panel

Each school and college gets login access to upload notices and events, manage student information and coordinate with the district.

Feature 10

Alumni Section

Alumni register and connect with current students for mentorship, guidance and real-world advice from their own institutions.

Feature 11

Notification System

Important announcements and updates from the district are shared directly with students, so nothing important is missed.

Feature 12

Admin Dashboard

The District Administration views total students, participation in activities, counselling requests and institution-wise data at a glance.

Feature 13

Language Support

Built to serve everyone — the portal supports English, Hindi and Urdu so language is never a barrier to getting help.

Feature 14

Data Security

A proper login system with data protection is maintained throughout — especially for sensitive counselling-related information.
